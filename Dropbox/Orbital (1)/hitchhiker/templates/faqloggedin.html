FAQs



Q: Who are the ones behind HitchHiker?

A: We are Team Anonymous from NUS Orbital 2015. You can find information about individual team members by clicking here.

Q: Do I have to sign up or register before I can use HitchHiker?

A: Yes, you will have to login with OpenID before you are able to make offers and requests on HitchHiker. We support login using OpenID for Yahoo, Flickr, AOL, Blogspot, Paypal, etc. If you are a current student at NUS, you can use https://openid.nus.edu.sg, which is the default option provided in the login box when you first visit the HitchHiker website. Simply click ‘Submit’ and you will be redirected to a page where you can log in using your NUSNET ID and password.

Q: Are there any fees that I have to pay when I make requests through HitchHiker?

A: No. HitchHiker is completely free to use but it is recommended that you split the petrol cost with the driver.

Q: How do I contact the other party who made an offer/request?

A: If you find an offer/request that match yours, you will be asked to provide your contact number through HitchHiker. The other party will get notified and contact you through the number you provided or the email address you use as your username.

Q: Why am I not able to edit/delete my offer/request in some cases?

A: You will not be able to edit/delete your offer/request once you use that offer/request to contact someone. If your offer/request has received a request/offer from others, you will not be able to edit/delete it either. You will have to personally contact and inform the other party if there are any changes. This is to prevent confusion for the other party.

Q: How safe is HitchHiker?

A: HitchHiker is a brand new carpooling website based in NUS and most of the users are likely to be current NUS students, making it a relatively safe and practical way to meet and make new friends!

Q: I have questions that are not answered here. How do I contact you?

A: You can drop us an email here.
